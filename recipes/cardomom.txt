Cardomom buns (based off King Arthur recipe)

* Make the dough

57 g butter (room temp)
280 g ap flour
57 g whole wheat
31 g sugar
6 g instant yeast
2 g ground cardomom
113 g warm water
85 g warm milk

1. Combine all ingredients in a stand mixer with hook attachment.

2. Mix for 1-2 minutes at low speed. Then 4-5 minutes at medium speed.

3. Transfer to bowl and place in proving drawer for 20 minutes.

4. Remove dough from proving drawer and kneed for 3-4 minutes until smooth.

5. Replace bowl in proving drawer and prove for 1 hour until roughly doubled in size.

* Make the filling and roll the dough

99 g sugar
5-8 g cardomom
1.5 t cinamon
1.5 t black cocoa

1 egg + 1 T water for wash

1. Combine filling ingrdients.

2. Remove dough from bowl and roll out to 20 in x 12 in rectangle. Brush with about half the wash.

3. Fold in a letter pattern longways.

4. Roll out to make a bit wider, roughly 8 x 16 in rectangle.

5. Cut long ways into 8 even strips. Twirl that shit and place on parchment paper.

* Second prove and bake

1. Place rolled buns in proving drawer for at least another hour. No more than 2 hours.

2. Preheat oven to 450 degrees when you're about 20 minutes from baking.

3. Pop them in the oven for 15-20 minutes.
