Ingredients
- 2 t yeast
- 225 g water
- 2 T olive oil
- 360 g flour (ap)
- 8 g salt (table/fine)

Method

1. Dissolve yeast in water with a little sugar for 15 minutes.

2. Mix all ingredients together in stand mixing bowl with a dough mixer. Then, run stand mixer for 4-5 minutes with dough hook.

3. Transfer dough to a large covered and greased bowl. Prove at 85 degrees for 90 minutes.

4. After there is a good rise, carefully trasnfer to tray that you will bake in. Stretch and let rest for at least 90 minutes. Keep covered.

5. Bake on a preheated stone at 450-475 degrees.
