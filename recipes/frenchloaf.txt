Ingredients
- 500 g ap flour
- 365 g water
- 11 g table salt
- 6 g yeast

Method

1. Make the sponge with yeast and a little water/sugar mix. Let sit for 5 minutes.

2. Mix all ingredients together until the dough is cohesive, yet shaggy. Do 20-30 dough folds.

3. Prove for 3 hours. Every 30 minutes fold the dough.
