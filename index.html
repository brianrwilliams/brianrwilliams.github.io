  • A holographic approach to the six-dimensional superconformal index. 2022. With Surya Raghavendran. arxiv.

    From the perspective of twisted holography we give a description of the space of local operators of the mysterious six-dimensional superconformal theory after the holomorphic twist. We find that the spaces of local operators assemble into representations for the exceptional linearly compact super Lie algebra E(3|6).
