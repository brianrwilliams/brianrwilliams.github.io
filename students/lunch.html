The geometry and physics lunch seminar
Fall 2025

This is a seminar series about topics in geometry and physics. The seminar is held every Wednesday from noon-1pm in CDS 524. You are encouraged to bring your lunch. If you would like to be added to the email list for this seminar, please reach out to bwill22@bu.edu.

The topics vary from foundational, classical, to cutting-edge new research. Typically, the topic will be framed by a particular article. You can see the current list of topics below. If you would like to have write-access to this list of topics, please reach out to bwill22@bu.edu.
